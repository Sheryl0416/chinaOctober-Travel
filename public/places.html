📍 Places to Visit – October China Trip

📍 ShenYang

Shenyang Imperial Palace 沈阳故宫

A UNESCO-listed site and one of the best-preserved Qing dynasty palaces. It's the second-largest imperial palace complex after Beijing's Forbidden City.

Zhong Jie 中街

The oldest commercial pedestrian street in China, filled with local food, shops, and traditional architecture. A great place for night walks and street snacks.

📍 Beijing

Forbidden City 北京故宫

The largest and most complete imperial palace in China. A historical treasure featuring thousands of rooms, courtyards, and ancient relics.

Great Wall of China 万里长城

One of the world’s greatest wonders and a UNESCO World Heritage site. Enjoy breathtaking views and hikes at famous sections like Badaling or Mutianyu.

Universal Studios Beijing 北京环球影城

A top theme park in China featuring attractions from Harry Potter, Jurassic World, Minions, and more. Great for thrilling rides and movie lovers!
